# PrimeNest Global Frontend Design System

## Brand Direction

Luxury editorial aesthetic inspired by:
* Dior Maison
* Louis Vuitton hospitality
* Aesop
* Loro Piana
* High-end Asian wellness brands

Mood:
* Quiet luxury
* Premium
* Minimal
* Modern
* Clean
* Structured
* Trustworthy

Avoid:
* Generic AI website aesthetic
* Overdesigned layouts
* Heavy gradients
* Neon colors
* Alibaba supplier styling
* Cheap supplement visuals
* Crowded sections

---

## Typography

Use elegant serif headlines paired with clean sans-serif body text.

Headlines:
* Large spacing
* Strong hierarchy
* Clean line breaks
* Luxury editorial feel

Body text:
* Easy readability
* Clean spacing
* Minimal clutter

Avoid random font sizing.

Use consistent typography scale.

---

## Layout System

Use:
* Large breathing space
* Structured grids
* Consistent spacing
* Premium whitespace
* Clean section separation

Use 8px spacing system.

Avoid cramped layouts.

---

## Color System

Primary palette:
* Warm black
* Champagne gold
* Soft ivory
* Warm beige
* Neutral stone tones

Avoid:
* Bright red
* Neon gold
* Pure black everywhere
* Harsh contrast

---

## Components

Buttons:
* Soft hover transitions
* Premium feel
* Rounded but refined
* Smooth animations

Cards:
* Soft shadows
* Elegant borders
* Editorial spacing
* Consistent hierarchy

Forms:
* Minimal
* Luxury styling
* Clear spacing
* High readability

---

## Animation Rules

Use Framer Motion for all animations.

Apply:
* Scroll fade reveals
* Staggered card reveals
* Smooth hover transitions
* Soft hero entrance animations

Animations should feel:
* Calm
* Premium
* Smooth
* Expensive

Avoid flashy motion.

---

## Product Photography Direction

Use:
* Realistic bird nest textures
* Warm natural lighting
* Editorial product framing
* Luxury hospitality mood

Avoid:
* CGI appearance
* Plastic textures
* Over-whitened nests
* Fake luxury props

Preserve:
* Natural fiber texture
* Handmade edges
* Rice-white tones
* Authentic realism

---

## UX Rules

Every section should support:
* Trust
* Clarity
* Conversion
* Source transparency

Prioritize:
* Mobile responsiveness
* Fast readability
* Strong CTA visibility
* Luxury presentation

Avoid:
* Too many CTA buttons
* Visual clutter
* Inconsistent spacing
* Random animations
