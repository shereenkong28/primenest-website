About PrimeNest Global 关于 PrimeNest Global

Direct From Our Malaysian Swiftlet Houses 源自马来西亚自营燕屋

PrimeNest Global owns and operates swiftlet houses in Malaysia. With over 20 years of swiftlet farming experience, we manage the supply from nest collection to wholesale-ready product. PrimeNest Global 在马来西亚经营自营燕屋，拥有20年以上燕屋养殖经验。我们管理从燕窝采收到批发供应的流程，确保货源稳定。

Our house nests are hand-cleaned by experienced workers without bleaching or chemical additives. Selected cave nests may require additional machine cleaning due to heavier natural mineral residue. 屋燕由经验工人手工挑毛清理，不漂白，不添加化学成分。部分洞燕因天然矿物残留较重，可能需要额外机器清洗处理。

  • 20+ years of swiftlet farming experience 20年以上燕屋养殖经验
  • Hand-cleaned house nests, no bleaching, no chemical additives 屋燕人工挑毛，不漂白，不添加化学成分

Priority Markets 重点市场

Markets We Serve 我们服务的市场

Supplying selected wholesale buyers across key Asian markets, with export enquiries welcome from other regions. 专注亚洲重点市场，同时欢迎其他国家与地区的采购咨询。

CN
China 中国 Mainland · Importers · Distributors 进口商 · 经销商 · 批发商

China remains a key market for Malaysian edible bird's nest, with demand from TCM retailers, gifting houses, distributors, and premium e-commerce channels. We support importers and licensed distributors with consistent grades and export-ready supply. 中国仍是马来西亚燕窝的重要市场，需求来自进口商、经销商、中药行及礼品渠道。我们提供稳定等级与出口供应。

SG
Singapore 新加坡 Distributors · TCM · Boutique Brands 经销商 · 中药行 · 精品品牌

Singapore is a quality-driven market with strong demand for clean, well-presented bird's nest. Our hand-cleaned, no-bleach grades are suited for TCM retailers, boutique gifting brands, wellness stores, and regional distributors. 新加坡市场重视清洁度、外观及可追溯性。我们的手工清洗、无漂白燕窝适合中药行、精品礼品品牌及健康零售渠道。

TW
Taiwan 台湾 Importers · Health Food · Gifting 进口商 · 健康食品 · 礼品市场

Taiwan has a mature consumer base for premium bird's nest, especially in postpartum care, gifting, and health food retail. We supply consistent grades suitable for long-term wholesale distribution. 台湾拥有成熟的高端燕窝消费市场，尤其集中在产后护理、送礼及健康食品渠道。我们提供稳定等级，适合长期批发合作。

BN
Brunei 文莱 Local Distributors · Wellness Retail 本地经销商 · 健康零售

Brunei is a close regional market with demand for clean, traceable bird's nest. Our Kelantan source supports shorter lead times, flexible order sizes, and direct supply to local distributors and wellness retailers. 文莱是邻近市场，对干净、可追溯燕窝有稳定需求。吉兰丹产地带来更快交期及更灵活的采购数量。
